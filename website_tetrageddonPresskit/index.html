Tetrageddon Games (and its downloadable desktop counterpart: ARMAGAD (also Tetrageddon Games)) is probably the weirdest most tortured not-a-game game you will ever experience.

It is the internet condensed into a slew of interactive experiences that serve as something of a love-letter to the web of the past.

The experience is thoroughly riddled with hidden extras and tangents. Much like a virtual arcade, it largely consists of a collection of games. Among others, these include: Anatomically Incorrect Dinosaurs, FROGGY (It's Hungry), and Electric Love Potatoes.

Individual games have been shown at events such as Mild Rumpus at GDC 2016 and Fantastic Arcade.

alienmelon (also known as Nathalie Lawhead in physical spaces) is a collective construct of everyone's mind. She only exists because everyone thinks that she is real. She began as an NPC (non-player character) in an early browser text-based game sometime in the 90's. The character was convincing enough that people eventually began inviting her to events, conferences, and other gatherings in RL. As is the case with computers, somehow some weird programming shit happened and she was able to start showing up in the physical world. She spends most of her days making games, but wishes to one day return to her virtual home, and continue being an NPC.
